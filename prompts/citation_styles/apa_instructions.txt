**APA (American Psychological Association) Style**

**In-Text Citations:**
- Format: (Author, Year) or (Author & Author, Year) or (Author et al., Year)
- Placement: Before the period at end of sentence
- Single author: (Smith, 2020)
- Two authors: (Smith & Jones, 2020)
- Three or more authors: (Smith et al., 2020)
- No page numbers for general paraphrasing
- With page numbers (direct quotes): (Smith, 2020, p. 15)

**References (end of document):**
- Format: Author, A. A. (Year). Title of work. Publisher.
- Alphabetical order by author last name
- Hanging indent (second line indented)

**Example:**
Smith, J. A., & Jones, B. C. (2020). Healthcare reform in America. Academic Press.
