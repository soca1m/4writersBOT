**MLA (Modern Language Association) Style**

**In-Text Citations:**
- Format: (Author Page) or (Author and Author Page)
- Placement: Before the period at end of sentence
- Single author: (Smith 45)
- Two authors: (Smith and Jones 45)
- Three or more authors: (Smith et al. 45)
- No comma between author and page
- No "p." or "pp." before page numbers

**Works Cited (end of document):**
- Format: Author Last Name, First Name. "Title of Work." Publication, Date.
- Alphabetical order by author last name
- Hanging indent (second line indented)

**Example:**
Smith, John A., and Barbara C. Jones. "Healthcare Reform in America." Journal of Medicine, vol. 15, no. 3, 2020, pp. 45-67.
