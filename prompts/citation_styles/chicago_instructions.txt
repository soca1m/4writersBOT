**Chicago (Author-Date) Style**

**In-Text Citations:**
- Format: (Author Year, Page) or (Author and Author Year, Page)
- Placement: Before the period at end of sentence
- Single author: (Smith 2020, 45)
- Two authors: (Smith and Jones 2020, 45)
- Three or more authors: (Smith et al. 2020, 45)
- Comma between year and page
- No "p." before page numbers

**References (end of document):**
- Format: Author Last Name, First Name. Year. Title of Work. Publisher.
- Alphabetical order by author last name
- Hanging indent (second line indented)

**Example:**
Smith, John A., and Barbara C. Jones. 2020. Healthcare Reform in America. Chicago: University of Chicago Press.
